Snoop Dogg

Cordozar Calvin Broadus Jr.aka. Snoop Dogg also called Snoop Doggy Dogg and Snoop Lion, is well known for his Gangster Rap. Snoop Dogg was born on October 20, 1971. he was born in Long Beach, California, U.S. Snoop dogg became one of the most known American rappers because of how popular his gangster rap music was.

Snoop Dogg's first hit song that put him on the map was his hit song Doggystyle. Snoop Dogg has accumulated Seven platnum albums such as, Da Game Is To Be Sold, Not To Be Told, No Limit Top Dogg, and Tha Doggfather Tha Last Meal (2000) ... and many other achievements such as, he received the Billboard Music Award in three categories; 'Top Billboard 200 Album Artist - Male', 'Top R&B Album Artist', and 'Top R&B Album Artist - Male'.
